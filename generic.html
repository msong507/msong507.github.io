Welcome to my digital portfolio. I'm Misun (Mia) Song, a data science professional whose roots are steeped in the business world. My journey reflects the harmonious blend of business strategy and data analytics - two fields that may seem distinct, yet are profoundly intertwined in shaping the businesses of the future.

After my successful career as a senior business Analyst at LG Fashion, my curiosity about the data driving sustainable business decisions led me towards the captivating field of data science. To equip myself with the necessary skills, I pursued a Master's in Applied Data Science from The University of Chicago, where I mastered languages like Python, R, SQL, and tools such as Tableau and Power BI. However, the most valuable lesson learned was how to integrate these data-driven insights with business strategies.

During my data analytics internship at Brackets, I applied the principle by creating a Tableau dashboard simulation and directing the development of strategic data strategy reports. This allowed me to impact client decisions and growth strategies by merging my data science proficiency with business insight. Similarly, in my capstone project, I revamped PowerKiosk's energy price prediction system, showcasing my dedication to leveraging data science for tackling intricate real-world challenges.

In addition to these achievements, I've founded Pivot to Data Science, a platform aimed at assisting individuals transitioning into the field, reflecting my commitment to foster a supportive data science community.

In this portfolio, you'll witness the confluence of my skills, experiences, and dedication to data science. My journey stands as a testament to my ability to contribute to various data science initiatives, and I hope it serves as an inspiration to you.
